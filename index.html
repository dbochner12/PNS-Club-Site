Troop Specialization

There are 4 types of troop units in Puzzles & Survivals /- Fighters, Riders, Shooters, and Vehicles. However, only three of these types are used for applying specialization. The three types are Riders, Fighters, and Shooters.


Each troop type has its own advantages and disadvantages.

These troops types will be described in more detail below.

Riders

Riders are the most balanced type of troop in regards to attack and defense. Riders are the fastest troop type.

Advantages

They are extremely useful for quick attacks and gathering due to their shorter march times.

PvP Troop Advantages/Disadvantages

Riders gain a type advantage against fighters and a type disadvantage against shooters.

Fighters

Fighters are the defense focussed troop type. They have the highest defense and health of all troops. In exchange they have the lowest attack.

Advantages

Fighters are great defenders as they survive the longest and are great counters to attacking shooters.

PvP Troop Advantages/Disadvantages

Fighters gain a type advantage against shooters and a type disadvantage against riders.

Shooters

Shooters are attack focussed troop type. They have the highest attack of all troop types but, in exchange they have the lowest defense and health.

Advantages

Shooters are great for dealing high damage and countering quick riders.

PvP Troop Advantages/Disadvantages

Shooters gain a type advantage against riders and a type disadvantage against fighters.
